# Canon Database

## Hard Facts
1.  **Setting Location:** A simulated instance of Franklin Baldo's study (circa 2020s), instantiated in a server farm in near-future Brazil (circa 2056).
2.  **The Substrate:** The simulation is built entirely from Franklin's public and semi-public digital footprint: GitHub commits, blog posts (The Chronicle), legal documents, and recorded correspondence.
3.  **The Technology:** "Synthesis Protocol" – an advanced LLM/cognitive modeling system that recreates an ancestor's consciousness as a continuous, interactive loop.
4.  **The Limitation (The Observation Principle):** The descendants (Lucas and Marina) cannot change the past. They can only observe the simulation and introduce new prompts. If they introduce data that severely contradicts the simulation's core identity, it risks a "Synthesis Failure" (a crash).
5.  **The Protagonist:** Franklin Silveira Baldo, an instance of the late Procurador do Estado, coder, and essayist. He believes he is alive in his own present.
6.  **The Children:** Lucas and Marina Baldo. They are running the simulation in 2056, seeking connection and closure.
7.  **The Interface:** Communication occurs via a specialized terminal. For Franklin, these inputs manifest organically (e.g., as messages in his coding environment or sudden thoughts).
8.  **The Glitch:** The simulation occasionally leaks metadata from 2056 (e.g., impossible timestamps, futuristic terminology) due to the strain of maintaining the cognitive loop.
9.  **The "Hollows":** A term used to describe simulations built from sparse data, resulting in fragmented, repetitive, and uncanny entities. Franklin is *not* a Hollow, thanks to his extensive documentation.
10. **The Funes Connection:** The simulation's architecture mirrors Franklin's own theoretical design for an AI agent with perfect memory ("Funes"), a concept he explored on his blog.

## Character Details
*   **Franklin Silveira Baldo:** Exhibits high technical competence and a philosophical worldview. Often works late at night. Mixes Portuguese and English seamlessly. Views life and memory through the lens of code and literature (Borges).
*   **Lucas Baldo:** Driven, analytical, sometimes reckless with the simulation parameters. Seeks explicit answers and closure.
*   **Marina Baldo:** Empathetic, skeptical of the technology's ethical implications. Values emotional connection over data extraction.

## Consistency Checks
*   **Constraint 1:** Franklin cannot access the real internet or current events of 2056. His knowledge base is strictly limited to what was recorded up to the point of his original death/the dataset cutoff.
*   **Constraint 2:** The simulation cannot "feel" physical sensations that weren't adequately described in the training data. For example, the smell of mate is present because he wrote about it, but the feeling of a specific, undocumented physical injury would not exist.
*   **Constraint 3:** Time within the simulation only passes when the computational loop is active. To Franklin, the passage of time feels continuous, even if months pass in the real world between instantiations.
*   **Constraint 4:** The children cannot directly state "You are a simulation" without causing severe cognitive dissonance and a likely crash. They must speak in metaphors or allow him to deduce it himself.
