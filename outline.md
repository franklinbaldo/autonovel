# Outline: The Siphon's Price

## Save the Cat Beat Sheet

*   **Opening Image (0-1%):** Kael stands in a damp, rust-stained room in the Lower Wards, carefully extracting a memory from a desperate mother. The vial glows amber. He hands her a few silver coins. She looks at the coins, her face already going slack with the loss.
*   **Theme Stated (~5%):** Lira, examining a vial of "dregs," tells Kael, "You can't hoard what you don't feel. Someday, you'll have to choose between your coin purse and your soul." Kael ignores her, focusing on his escape fund for Elara.
*   **Setup (1-10%):** Establish Kael's routine as a black-market Siphoner. Show the stark contrast between the Hollows of the Basin and the Cortex Elite above. Establish Kael's overwhelming protective nature towards his 14-year-old brother, Elara, who is kept hidden. Kael believes he is close to buying their way out of Oakhaven.
*   **Catalyst (~11%):** An Enforcer raid on the black market goes wrong. A high-ranking Cortex Elite's son is killed in the crossfire, and a highly unstable, illegal memory vial (a "black vial" containing a weaponized traumatic memory) is lost. The Enforcers begin a brutal sweep of the Basin, targeting all Siphoners.
*   **Debate (11-23%):** Kael realizes his stash is no longer safe. Lira approaches him with a dangerous proposition: The Blank Slate cult wants him to help steal the central memory ledger of the Cortex Elite to blackmail them into stopping the sweep. Kael refuses, wanting only to run with Elara.
*   **Break Into Two (~23%):** The Enforcers raid Kael's safehouse. He and Elara barely escape, but Kael loses his entire stash of memory vials—years of horrific work, gone. With no money to escape and the Enforcers hunting him, Kael realizes he has no choice but to take Lira's job. He crosses the threshold into the violent politics of Oakhaven.
*   **B Story (~27%):** Kael and Lira are forced to work together. Her idealism (wanting to destroy the system) clashes with his pragmatism (wanting only to survive). Their dynamic begins to challenge Kael's lie that he must remain detached.
*   **Fun and Games (26-50%):** The "heist" preparation. Kael and Lira navigate the treacherous underworld of the Siphon Cartels to gather gear and intel. They pull off small, dangerous jobs to infiltrate the Upper Wards. Kael's surgical skills are tested in combat scenarios. They witness the grotesque decadence of the Elite, bloated on stolen memories.
*   **Midpoint (~50%):** False Victory. They successfully infiltrate a lower-level vault and secure a key to the central ledger. But a complication arises: Elara, trying to prove he isn't a burden, follows them and is captured by Magistrate Vane's personal guard. The stakes are instantly elevated from survival to rescue.
*   **Bad Guys Close In (50-68%):** Vane realizes who Kael is and what he's planning. He tightens his grip on the Basin. The Blank Slate cult, feeling Kael is compromised by Elara's capture, threatens to turn on him. Kael and Lira are hunted from both sides. Kael's cold demeanor cracks as desperation sets in.
*   **All Is Lost (~68%):** Kael and Lira are cornered by Enforcers. To save Lira and the mission, Kael allows himself to be captured, sacrificing his own freedom. He is brought before Magistrate Vane, who reveals he intends to "Complete Siphon" Elara as punishment for Kael's crimes. Kael faces the ultimate failure: his brother's hollowing.
*   **Dark Night of the Soul (68-77%):** In a holding cell, awaiting the needle, Kael confronts his ghost and his lie. He realizes that by shutting himself off and treating everything as a transaction, he inadvertently brought this upon Elara. He accepts his need: he must fight not just to survive, but to dismantle the system that created him, even if it costs him his own memories.
*   **Break Into Three (~77%):** Lira, having rallied the remaining Blank Slate members using her Whisperer abilities, breaches the Enforcer compound. She brings Kael his specialized, black-market Siphon gear.
*   **Finale (77-97%):**
    *   *(a) Gather the Team:* Kael, Lira, and the rebels coordinate an assault on Vane's central extraction facility.
    *   *(b) Execute the Plan:* They fight their way through the Enforcers, using volatile "dregs" as makeshift chemical weapons.
    *   *(c) High Tower Surprise:* They reach Vane's inner sanctum, but Vane has already begun extracting Elara. He also reveals he controls the "black vial" lost in the Catalyst, ready to use it on the rebels.
    *   *(d) Dig Deep Down:* Kael must face Vane in brutal, close-quarters combat while avoiding the Siphon machinery. He is forced to weaponize his own Siphon against Vane, injecting him with a massive dose of raw, unprocessed trauma (dregs) to incapacitate him.
    *   *(e) Execute the New Plan:* Kael frees Elara, stopping the extraction just in time. Lira destroys the central memory ledger, plunging the elite into chaos and fulfilling the rebel goal.
*   **Final Image (~99%):** Kael, bloodied and exhausted, stands at the rim of the crater with Elara and Lira. They have no money, but they are free. Kael looks at his brother, not with detached protection, but with genuine, painful love. He drops his Siphon needle over the edge into the Basin.

## Chapter-by-Chapter Progression

### Act I: The Basin (1-5)
*   **Ch 1:** Establish Kael's brutal trade and the setting of the Lower Wards. Show the physical cost of extraction on a desperate client. Introduce Elara and Kael's overwhelming need to protect him.
*   **Ch 2:** Introduce Lira and the Whisperers. She appraises Kael's stash, hinting at the gathering political storm. Theme is stated.
*   **Ch 3:** The Catalyst. The Enforcer raid tears through the market. Kael barely escapes, setting up the immediate physical threat.
*   **Ch 4:** The Debate. Lira pitches the heist to Kael. He refuses, preferring to flee. He attempts to finalize his escape plans.
*   **Ch 5:** The Break Into Two. Enforcers raid Kael's home. He loses his stash. Cornered and broke, he agrees to Lira's plan.

### Act II: The Climb (6-16)
*   **Ch 6:** Kael and Lira enter the deeper underworld. B-Story begins as their conflicting worldviews clash during a dangerous negotiation with a cartel boss.
*   **Ch 7:** A "try-fail" cycle. They attempt to acquire schematics for the Upper Wards from a corrupt Enforcer. The deal goes sour, resulting in a violent escape. (Yes, but... they escape, but are now marked).
*   **Ch 8:** Infiltration prep. Kael must perform a delicate, illegal surgery to forge identification implants. Lira uses her Whisperer skills to gather passwords from discarded elite vials.
*   **Ch 9:** First look at the Upper Wards. The sickening contrast of the Cortex Elite's decadence. Kael struggles to maintain his detached facade when he sees what the stolen memories are used for.
*   **Ch 10:** The Midpoint infiltration. They successfully breach a secondary vault and steal a critical access key.
*   **Ch 11:** The Midpoint complication. Elara, having snuck out to help, is caught by Vane's guards outside the vault. The stakes pivot sharply.
*   **Ch 12:** Bad Guys Close In. Vane begins torturing Elara (psychologically) to draw Kael out. The Blank Slate rebels threaten to abandon Kael, seeing him as a liability.
*   **Ch 13:** A desperate "No, and..." scene. Kael attempts a solo rescue of Elara, but walks into a trap. He barely survives, and Elara's situation worsens.
*   **Ch 14:** Lira confronts Kael about his isolation. They must rely on each other to survive the Enforcer sweeps. Trust is finally built.
*   **Ch 15:** The All Is Lost moment. During a massive Enforcer crackdown on the rebel hideout, Kael surrenders himself to buy time for Lira to escape.
*   **Ch 16:** Dark Night of the Soul. Kael in captivity. Vane reveals his plan for Elara. Kael breaks down, confronting his lie and accepting his need to fight the system, not just run from it.

### Act III: The Shattering (17-21)
*   **Ch 17:** Break Into Three. Lira leads the rebels in a chaotic assault on the holding facility, freeing Kael.
*   **Ch 18:** The assault on Vane's central extraction tower. High-action, utilizing the specific geography and magic system (using memory fluid as a weapon).
*   **Ch 19:** The climax begins. They reach the inner sanctum. Vane has Elara hooked up to the Siphon machine.
*   **Ch 20:** The final confrontation. Kael vs. Vane. Kael uses his Siphoning skills offensively to defeat Vane. Lira destroys the ledger.
*   **Ch 21:** Resolution. The immediate aftermath. The elite economy is shattered. Kael, Elara, and Lira stand at the crater's edge, facing an uncertain but free future.

## Foreshadowing Ledger

*   **Thread 1: The "Black Vial"**
    *   *Setup (Ch 3):* Mentioned during the Catalyst raid as a highly unstable, weaponized memory lost by the elite.
    *   *Reminder (Ch 9):* Lira hears rumors in the Upper Wards that Vane is obsessed with recovering a "weaponized trauma."
    *   *Payoff (Ch 19):* Vane produces the Black Vial during the climax, intending to use it on the rebels.
*   **Thread 2: Kael's Mother's Death**
    *   *Setup (Ch 1):* Kael briefly remembers his mother wandering into the Rust Barrens after selling her memories.
    *   *Reminder (Ch 12):* Vane taunts Kael, implying he knows what happened to his mother.
    *   *Payoff (Ch 16):* During the Dark Night, Kael realizes his detachment is exactly what caused his mother to sell her memories in the first place—he didn't let her lean on him.
*   **Thread 3: The Danger of "Dregs"**
    *   *Setup (Ch 2):* Lira explains that consuming grey, degraded memories (dregs) causes violent psychosis.
    *   *Reminder (Ch 8):* They witness a Hollow in the Basin going mad after consuming a bad batch.
    *   *Payoff (Ch 18/20):* Kael weaponizes a massive dose of dregs during the assault, using it to incapacitate Vane's guards and eventually Vane himself.
*   **Thread 4: Elara's Independence**
    *   *Setup (Ch 1 & 4):* Elara expresses a desire to help, but Kael shuts him down, insisting he stay hidden.
    *   *Reminder (Ch 7):* Elara is shown secretly observing the Siphon Cartels from a distance.
    *   *Payoff (Ch 11):* Elara attempts to help during the Midpoint infiltration and is captured, driving the second half of the plot.
