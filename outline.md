# Chapter Outline: The City of Broken Mirrors

## Act I: The Marrow-Thief (0-23%)
### Chapter 1: The Descent (Opening Image)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen, a skilled marrow-thief, steals a vial of pure marrow-oil from a House Vane transport in the upper Spine, barely escaping a squad of guards. He uses Osteomancy to bend a rib-bridge, feeling the dull ache in his own skeleton.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Adrenaline to desperate relief, then dread as he feels his bones weaken.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... He gets the oil, but the guards recognize his unique humming frequency.
*   **Foreshadowing:** He notices the bone of the bridge is unusually warm and a faint, rhythmic vibration that isn't the wind. (Plant 1: The Leviathan's heartbeat).

### Chapter 2: The Rot (Setup)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen returns to the Rib Slums and gives the oil to Elara, whose Marrow-Rot is worsening. She asks him about a memory of their mother, and he realizes he's forgotten her face—a consequence of a past extraction.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Guilt and determination.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** No, and... The oil barely eases Elara's pain, and he realizes he needs something stronger, something deeper.

### Chapter 3: The Rumor of the Core (Theme Stated)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen meets with his fence, Jax, who tells him about a massive, unmined vein of pure, ancient marrow deep in the leviathan's skull—the Deep Core. Jax warns him it's suicide; the bone there is "alive."
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Intrigue shifting to reckless hope.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... He gets the location, but Jax demands a steep cut and warns him the Cult of the Waking God guards it.

### Chapter 4: The Archivist's Offer (Catalyst)
*   **Summary:** The Archivist, leader of the Hollow Syndicate, corners Kaelen. She knows he's going for the Deep Core and offers to fund the expedition in exchange for a specific, ancient memory trapped in the skull's marrow.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Suspicion and reluctant acceptance.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... He gets the gear he needs, but he is now indebted to the most dangerous crime lord in Ostrum, and the price is high.

### Chapter 5: The Descent Begins (Debate / Break Into Two)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen wrestles with the danger, knowing the extraction could shatter his bones or erase his core identity. He looks at Elara's worsening condition and chooses to descend into the treacherous Deep Core, crossing the threshold from the known slums into the perilous unknown.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Fear transitioning to absolute resolve.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, and... He successfully bypasses the first layer of House Vane security and enters the dark, unmapped tunnels of the lower skull.

## Act II: The Deep Core (23-77%)
### Chapter 6: The Singing Bone (B-Story / Fun and Games)
*   **Summary:** Deep in the skull, Kaelen encounters Lyra, a rogue Osteomancer from the Cult of the Waking God. She is surprisingly young and uses a bizarre, discordant form of bone-singing. She claims she isn't mining marrow; she's "soothing" the Pale God.
*   **POV:** Lyra
*   **Emotional Arc:** Curiosity and cautious alliance.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** No, but... Kaelen tries to sneak past her, fails, but they form a tense truce when House Vane guards arrive.

### Chapter 7: The Heat of the Marrow (Fun and Games)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen and Lyra navigate the treacherous tunnels, avoiding massive marrow-hounds. The bone here is hot to the touch, and the rhythmic vibrations are unmistakable. Kaelen realizes the "earthquakes" are the leviathan breathing.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Awe and mounting terror.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... They find the ancient vein, but the heat is suffocating, and the marrow is guarded by Shattered Osteomancers loyal to House Vane.

### Chapter 8: The Extraction (Midpoint)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen reaches the vein. He presses his ear to the hot marrow and sings the Note of Recall to extract the memory the Archivist wants. The memory hits him with staggering force: it is not a human memory. It is the leviathan's memory of dying, and its sudden realization that it is waking up.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Overwhelming cosmic horror and physical agony.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... He gets the memory (and a vial of pure oil for Elara), but the extraction costs him the memory of his own name, and his left arm partially crystallizes into Shatterglass.

### Chapter 9: The Chase (Bad Guys Close In)
*   **Summary:** House Vane guards, alerted by the massive surge of Osteomancy, corner them. Lord Vane himself, carried on a palanquin, demands the memory, realizing Kaelen holds the secret to the city's destruction.
*   **POV:** Lyra
*   **Emotional Arc:** Panic and desperate survival.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** No, and... They fail to escape quietly, and Kaelen's shattered arm makes fighting nearly impossible.

### Chapter 10: The Shattered Arm (Bad Guys Close In)
*   **Summary:** A brutal fight ensues. Kaelen is forced to use Osteomancy with his shattered arm, fracturing it further. Lyra uses her discordant singing to collapse a tunnel, separating them from Vane, but trapping them deeper.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Agony and the realization of his own fragility.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... They escape immediate capture, but are lost in the darkest part of the skull, and Kaelen is severely injured.

### Chapter 11: The Hollow's Betrayal (All Is Lost)
*   **Summary:** They finally emerge near the Rib Slums, only to be ambushed by Jax, who sold them out to House Vane. Jax steals the vial of pure marrow-oil meant for Elara, leaving Kaelen bleeding and broken.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Absolute despair. The scent of death (literal, as his arm rots, and metaphorical, for Elara).
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** No, and... He loses the cure, he is betrayed, and he realizes his Lie (that he can survive alone by stealing) has destroyed him.

### Chapter 12: The Waking God (Dark Night of the Soul)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen collapses in the slums. He holds the leviathan's memory sphere. He realizes that House Vane's continuous mining is the only thing keeping the city afloat, but it's also the very thing waking the beast that will destroy them all. He accepts his Need: he cannot just save Elara; he must stop the mining, even if it means the city falls, to save anyone.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Despair turning into a grim, self-sacrificial acceptance.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** N/A (Internal revelation).

### Chapter 13: The Note of Breaking (Break Into Three)
*   **Summary:** Lyra helps him back to his feet. She reveals that the Cult of the Waking God knows a "Note of Breaking"—a frequency that can shatter the leviathan's spine, permanently paralyzing it before it can fully wake and destroy the city, but it requires massive Osteomancy power.
*   **POV:** Lyra
*   **Emotional Arc:** Hope, tainted by the massive risk.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, and... They form a plan to infiltrate the upper Spine and use the Note of Breaking, using the Archivist's resources.

## Act III: The Shattering (77-100%)
### Chapter 14: The Assault on the Spine (Finale - Gather the Team)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen returns to the Archivist. He gives her the leviathan's memory. In exchange, she provides the Syndicate's forces to stage a distraction against House Vane while Kaelen and Lyra infiltrate the Ivory Ward.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Cold determination.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... The Syndicate attacks, but Vane's guards are heavily fortified, and Kaelen's shattered arm makes climbing the spine excruciating.

### Chapter 15: The Ivory Palace (Finale - Execute the Plan)
*   **Summary:** Kaelen and Lyra reach the top of the spine. They confront Lord Vane's elite guard of Shattered Osteomancers. Kaelen uses his own brittle bones as weapons, breaking pieces of his shattered arm to form bone-daggers.
*   **POV:** Lyra
*   **Emotional Arc:** High-stakes action and brutal sacrifice.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, but... They defeat the guards, but Lord Vane captures Lyra, holding a bone-blade to her throat.

### Chapter 16: The High Tower Surprise (Finale - Twist)
*   **Summary:** Lord Vane reveals he knew the leviathan was waking all along. He plans to use the massive surge of energy from its awakening to cast a spell of Osteomancy that will make him functionally immortal, sacrificing the entire city in the process. He shatters Lyra's legs, neutralizing her.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Shock, horror, and burning rage.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** No, and... Vane begins the ritual, the city begins to shake violently, and Lyra is incapacitated.

### Chapter 17: The Final Chord (Finale - Dig Deep Down / Execute New Plan)
*   **Summary:** With Lyra down, Kaelen realizes he must sing the Note of Breaking himself. He knows it will cost him all his remaining memories and likely shatter his entire skeleton. He looks down at the crumbling city, thinks of Elara, and sings the chord, pouring every ounce of his life force into the bone.
*   **POV:** Kaelen
*   **Emotional Arc:** Ultimate sacrifice and profound peace.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** Yes, and... The Note resonates through the spine. Lord Vane's ritual is disrupted, and Vane is consumed by the backlash of his own magic, his body turning to dust. The leviathan shudders and goes still, permanently paralyzed.

### Chapter 18: The Hollow Hero (Final Image)
*   **Summary:** The city is saved, but severely damaged. The mining stops. Kaelen is found in the ruins of the Ivory Ward. His bones are entirely Shatterglass, and he is a complete Hollow, possessing zero memories of his past, not even his sister's name. Elara finds him. She survived, and though he doesn't know her, he feels an instinctive pull to protect her. The opening image of a desperate thief stealing a memory is mirrored by a man who has given all his memories to give the city a future.
*   **POV:** Elara
*   **Emotional Arc:** Bittersweet sorrow and hopeful resilience.
*   **Try-Fail Cycle:** N/A. The climax is resolved.

# Foreshadowing Ledger
1.  **The Leviathan's Heartbeat (Plant):** Ch 1 - Kaelen notices the bridge is warm and vibrating rhythmically.
    *   **Payoff:** Ch 7 - Kaelen realizes the "earthquakes" are the beast breathing and it is waking up.
2.  **The Brittle Arm (Plant):** Ch 8 - Kaelen's arm partially crystallizes into Shatterglass after extracting the massive memory.
    *   **Payoff:** Ch 15 - Kaelen breaks off pieces of his own Shatterglass arm to use as weapons against Vane's guards.
3.  **The Note of Breaking (Plant):** Ch 6 - Lyra uses a discordant form of bone-singing to "soothe" the bone.
    *   **Payoff:** Ch 17 - Kaelen uses this exact discordant note, amplified to a massive scale, to paralyze the leviathan's spine.
