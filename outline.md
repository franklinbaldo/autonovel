# Outline

## Structure
Total chapters: 15
Act breakdown: Act 1 (Ch 1-4), Act 2 (Ch 5-11), Act 3 (Ch 12-15)
Target word count: ~75,000 words

## Themes
- **The Cost of Survival:** What are we willing to lose to keep breathing?
- **Identity vs. Experience:** Are we more than the sum of what we remember?
- **Class and Exploitation:** The wealthy hoarding life while the poor burn it away.

## Act 1: The Ash and the Ledger
### Ch 1: "The Price of a Fire" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Opening Image: Elian in the freezing Slips, staring at a dying ember, trying to choose a memory to burn.
  2. Setup: Elian trades the memory of a childhood song for a burst of heat. He feels the immediate, cold hollow in his chest where the song used to live.
  3. Catalyst: Silas Thorne offers Elian a dangerous, highly illegal job: skimming emotional resonance from a high-society Keeper event.
- PLANTS: (foreshadowing seeded here)
  - Elian notices Taran humming a tune he can't quite place (payoff: Ch 12)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 2: "The Ward of Glass" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Debate: Elian refuses Silas at first, knowing the penalty is the White Room.
  2. The B-Story: Taran shows Elian a meager collection of "sweet" memories he's been saving, revealing he's skipping meals to hoard them.
  3. Break Into Two: Elian accepts Silas's job to protect Taran from having to burn his own meager cache.
- PLANTS:
  - Mention of the Magistrate's heavy perfume, smelling faintly of ozone and old blood (payoff: Ch 10)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 3: "Skimming the Surface" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Fun and Games: Elian infiltrates the Keepers' gala. The sensory overload of the Ward of Glass (music, silk, roast meats) is overwhelming after the Slips.
  2. The Heist: Elian successfully "skims" resonance from a Keeper boasting about his latest Maker-designed experience.
- PLANTS:
  - Elian spots Magistrate Vane talking intently with a terrified Maker (payoff: Ch 6)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 4: "The Echo" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Complication: Silas demands the skimmed resonance, but Elian realizes it's tied to a memory of a political assassination, not a simple pleasure.
  2. The Threshold: Elian must decide whether to hand over proof of a Keeper conspiracy or destroy it to protect himself.
- HARVESTS: (foreshadowing paid off here)
  - None yet.
- STATUS: unwritten

## Act 2: The Cinder Market
### Ch 5: "The Debt Collector" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Threat: The Cinder Syndicate demands the resonance. Silas reveals he knows about Taran's hidden stash.
  2. The Deal: Elian agrees to work directly for Silas to pay off the perceived debt, diving deeper into the underground economy of memories.
- PLANTS:
  - Silas's house is filled with dusty, unused women's clothing (payoff: Ch 14)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 6: "The Magistrate's Shadow" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Midpoint (False Victory): Elian completes a major job for Silas, earning enough "clean" memories to seemingly secure Taran's future.
  2. The Twist: Magistrate Vane raids the Cinder Market, capturing several of Elian's contacts. The stakes are raised; Elian is now a known target.
- HARVESTS:
  - The terrified Maker from Ch 3 is found dead in Vane's custody, having burned himself out completely.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 7: "The Hollow Men" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Bad Guys Close In: Vane tightens her grip on the Slips, demanding a "Memory Tithe" from the poorest citizens.
  2. The Consequence: Elian sees neighbors forced into becoming Hollowed just to meet the tithe. The horror of full memory loss is displayed.
- PLANTS:
  - Taran begins asking about their mother, a topic Elian violently avoids (payoff: Ch 13)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 8: "The White Room" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Capture: Elian is caught during a skimming operation by Vane's enforcers.
  2. The Interrogation: Vane tries to force Elian to reveal Silas's network. She uses sensory deprivation to starve him of magical fuel.
- HARVESTS:
  - None yet.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 9: "The Escape" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Sacrifice: Elian burns a precious, deep memory (the day Taran was born) to shatter the White Room's door.
  2. The Cost: He escapes, but forgets why he cares so much about the boy waiting for him in the Slips.
- PLANTS:
  - Elian finds a carved wooden bird in his pocket and doesn't know what it is (payoff: Ch 15)
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 10: "The True Cost" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. All Is Lost: Elian returns to Taran, treating him coldly, almost like a stranger. The emotional bond is severed.
  2. The Whiff of Death: Silas arrives, revealing he set Elian up to take the fall for the Syndicate's larger operation. Taran is taken as collateral.
- HARVESTS:
  - The smell of ozone and blood from Ch 2 is explained: Vane uses torture-memories to power the city wards, making her as corrupt as the Syndicate.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 11: "Dark Night of the Soul" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. The Realization: Elian, sitting in the cold Slips, realizes he has burned away the reason he was fighting in the first place. He has become exactly what he feared: hollow.
  2. The Decision: He chooses to act not out of remembered love, but out of a present choice to do right. He will rescue the boy he doesn't fully remember loving.
- HARVESTS:
  - None yet.
- STATUS: unwritten

## Act 3: The Burn
### Ch 12: "Gathering the Sparks" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Break Into Three: Elian plans an assault on Silas's stronghold. He needs immense power but has few memories left.
  2. The B-Story Convergence: He realizes he must use the "skimmed" resonance he stole in Ch 3, risking his own sanity.
- HARVESTS:
  - Elian hears a Hollowed humming the tune from Ch 1, realizing it was his mother's lullaby, giving him a fleeting anchor to use the skimmed magic safely.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 13: "The Syndicate's Fall" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Execute the Plan: Elian infiltrates the stronghold, using the overwhelming, chaotic resonance of the assassinated Keeper to blast through the defenses.
  2. The Confrontation: He faces Silas, who tries to force Elian into a memory burn.
- HARVESTS:
  - Taran's questions about their mother (Ch 7) surface; Elian uses the *absence* of that memory as a shield against Silas's attack.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 14: "The Magistrate's Choice" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. High Tower Surprise: Magistrate Vane arrives with her enforcers, intending to execute both Silas and Elian.
  2. Dig Deep Down: Elian exposes Vane's use of torture-memories (the secret from Ch 10) to her own men, breaking her authority.
- HARVESTS:
  - Silas's house of women's clothing (Ch 5) is destroyed in the crossfire; Silas breaks down, realizing he burned his wife for nothing, finally feeling the grief he avoided.
- STATUS: unwritten

### Ch 15: "The Ember" (POV: Elian)
- BEATS:
  1. Execute the New Plan: Elian secures Taran. He chooses *not* to burn his final memory of the escape to cover their tracks, accepting the risk to stay human.
  2. Final Image: Elian and Taran leaving Oakhaven. Elian looks at Taran, not remembering their past, but actively choosing to build a new one. The carved wooden bird is in his hand, a promise of new memories.
- HARVESTS:
  - The carved wooden bird (Ch 9) is revealed to be a toy Taran made for Elian, anchoring their new beginning.
- STATUS: unwritten
