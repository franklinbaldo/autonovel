# Character Registry: The Mnemic Wastes

## KAEL, The Siphoner (Protagonist)

*   **Role:** Black market memory extractor operating in the Lower Wards of Oakhaven.
*   **The Three Sliders:**
    *   Proactivity: 7 (He survives by hunting down high-value memories and staying ahead of the law)
    *   Likability: 4 (He is clinical, cold, and profits off desperation, but he protects his younger brother)
    *   Competence: 8 (He is a skilled, steady-handed surgeon with a deep understanding of the black market)

*   **Wound / Want / Need / Lie Framework:**
    *   **Ghost (Backstory):** When Kael was twelve, his mother sold her memory of his face to feed him and his infant brother, Elara. She looked at him as a stranger and soon after, wandered into the Rust Barrens and died.
    *   **Wound:** He feels responsible for his mother's hollowing and death. He is terrified of intimacy and attachment because he knows love is the first thing desperate people sell.
    *   **Lie:** He believes that survival requires him to remain utterly detached and unfeeling. "Memories are just fluid; we are just vessels." He believes he can buy back his humanity once he and his brother escape Oakhaven.
    *   **Want (External Goal):** To earn enough high-grade memory vials to bribe their way out of the city and into the rumored safe haven beyond the Rust Barrens.
    *   **Need (Internal Truth):** To accept that his mother's sacrifice wasn't his fault, and that closing himself off to protect Elara is slowly hollowing him out without a needle. He must learn to value the connections he has *now*.

*   **Dialogue Distinctiveness:**
    *   **Vocabulary Level:** Clinical, precise. He uses the terminology of his trade ("cortex," "dregs," "siphon").
    *   **Sentence Structure:** Terse, direct, and rarely uses contractions when giving instructions.
    *   **Verbal Tic:** Frequently asks, "Are you sure?" to shift the moral weight of the transaction onto the client.
    *   **Directness:** Highly direct. Never uses euphemisms for his work.

*   **Example Dialogue:**
    *   "Hold still. If you twitch, the needle hits the brainstem and you won't need the coin. Are you sure about this?"
    *   "It's just fluid. Don't romanticize it. By tomorrow, you won't even know what you lost."

## LIRA, The Whisperer (Deuteragonist)

*   **Role:** A street-smart appraiser in the Lower Wards, born with the rare mutation to "hear" the emotional resonance of empty vials.
*   **The Three Sliders:**
    *   Proactivity: 8 (She actively seeks out Kael for a dangerous job that will change the city's power dynamic)
    *   Likability: 7 (She is charismatic, sharp-tongued, and fiercely loyal to her fellow Hollows)
    *   Competence: 6 (She is a master appraiser but physically vulnerable and a poor fighter)

*   **Wound / Want / Need / Lie Framework:**
    *   **Ghost (Backstory):** Her father was a political dissident whose entire lifetime of memories was extracted as punishment. She grew up watching a drooling husk that looked like him sweeping the streets.
    *   **Wound:** She feels powerless against the systemic cruelty of the Cortex Elite and the Enforcers.
    *   **Lie:** She believes that the only way to beat the elite is to play by their rules, amassing enough wealth and secrets to buy her own army or influence.
    *   **Want (External Goal):** To organize the ultimate heist—stealing the city's central ledger of memories to blackmail the elite and fund a massive uprising of the Blank Slate.
    *   **Need (Internal Truth):** To realize that replacing one corrupt power structure with another won't save her people. She must learn that true rebellion requires dismantling the system entirely, not exploiting it.

*   **Dialogue Distinctiveness:**
    *   **Vocabulary Level:** Street slang mixed with poetic descriptions of the memories she senses.
    *   **Sentence Structure:** Fast, overlapping, and full of questions. She rarely stops talking.
    *   **Verbal Tic:** Hums a tuneless, repetitive melody when she's stressed or appraising a vial.
    *   **Directness:** Indirect. She talks around her true motives, testing people before she trusts them.

*   **Example Dialogue:**
    *   "This one? Smells like... burnt sugar and a bruised knee. A first kiss, maybe. Grade B. The elite'll pay three silvers for the nostalgia, but the dregs are murky. Not worth the risk, Kael."
    *   "You think you're outside the system because you hold the needle. Cute. But you're just as stuck in the mud as the rest of us."

## MAGISTRATE VANE (Antagonist)

*   **Role:** The ruthless head of the Cortex Elite's supply chain. He controls the legal extraction banks and the Enforcers.
*   **The Three Sliders:**
    *   Proactivity: 9 (He is constantly hunting down black market siphons and consolidating his power)
    *   Likability: 2 (He is deeply cynical, casually cruel, and views human beings entirely as livestock)
    *   Competence: 9 (He is a brilliant strategist and a terrifyingly efficient administrator)

*   **Wound / Want / Need / Lie Framework:**
    *   **Ghost (Backstory):** He grew up in the Lower Wards and clawed his way to the top by selling the memories of his own siblings to the elite. He is fundamentally terrified of returning to poverty.
    *   **Wound:** He hates himself for what he did to survive, but buries that hatred under a facade of aristocratic superiority.
    *   **Lie:** He believes that humanity is inherently selfish, and therefore, the strong are justified in consuming the weak. He tells himself he is doing the city a favor by maintaining order through the memory economy.
    *   **Want (External Goal):** To completely eradicate the black market and institute mandatory memory extraction quotas for the Lower Wards.
    *   **Need (Internal Truth):** To face the horrific reality of his own actions and recognize that order built on suffering is just a slower form of chaos. (Note: As an antagonist, he likely has a flat or negative arc, refusing this truth until it destroys him).

*   **Dialogue Distinctiveness:**
    *   **Vocabulary Level:** Highly educated, archaic, and excessively formal.
    *   **Sentence Structure:** Long, languid, and grammatically perfect. He never raises his voice.
    *   **Verbal Tic:** Pauses mid-sentence to sip from a vial of imported memory fluid, savoring the taste.
    *   **Directness:** Frighteningly direct, but delivered with polite, bureaucratic detachment.

*   **Example Dialogue:**
    *   "The rabble in the Basin misunderstand our arrangement. We do not steal their joy. We purchase it. It is a transaction. And like all transactions, it must be regulated."
    *   "You bleed out their childhoods for a few coppers, Kael. I take them to build a civilization. Do not confuse yourself with a surgeon. You are a butcher with clean hands."

## ELARA (Supporting)

*   **Role:** Kael's younger brother (14 years old). An innocent in a brutal world, fiercely protected by Kael.
*   **The Three Sliders:**
    *   Proactivity: 4 (He is mostly kept hidden and safe, but secretly tries to help Kael by running small errands)
    *   Likability: 9 (He is genuinely kind, observant, and refuses to let the city break his spirit)
    *   Competence: 3 (He is physically weak and lacks the ruthless survival skills of his brother)

*   **Wound / Want / Need / Lie Framework:**
    *   **Ghost (Backstory):** He has no memory of their mother, only Kael's grim stories. He feels the suffocating weight of Kael's overprotection.
    *   **Wound:** He feels useless and like a burden to Kael.
    *   **Lie:** He believes he must prove his worth to Kael by contributing to their escape fund, even if it means putting himself in danger.
    *   **Want (External Goal):** To find a way to earn memory vials on his own so Kael can stop butchering people.
    *   **Need (Internal Truth):** To realize that his true value to Kael isn't financial, but emotional—he is the only thing keeping Kael tethered to his humanity.

*   **Dialogue Distinctiveness:**
    *   **Vocabulary Level:** Simple, but observant. He notices details Kael ignores.
    *   **Sentence Structure:** Earnest, often asking questions. He uses contractions frequently.
    *   **Verbal Tic:** Stutters slightly when he is caught in a lie or trying to hide something from Kael.
    *   **Directness:** Direct and naive. He lacks the cynicism of the adults around him.

*   **Example Dialogue:**
    *   "I-I didn't go near the Siphon dens, Kael, I promise. I just wanted to see the sun hit the rim of the crater. It looked like gold."
    *   "You look at me like I'm made of glass. But I'm not. I can help. You don't have to do this alone."
