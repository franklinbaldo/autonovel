# Characters

## Elian Vance (POV)
- **Role:** Protagonist, an unlicensed spellcaster living in the Slips.
- **Age:** 26
- **Ghost:** He burned the memory of his mother's face to save his younger brother from freezing to death during the long winter of '94.
- **Wound:** He feels a constant, hollow ache where the memory of his mother used to be. He knows he loved her, but the feeling itself is gone.
- **Lie:** "I can burn the rest of my past as long as I keep my brother safe. The past is just fuel."
- **Want:** To earn enough money (or gather enough potent memories) to buy passage out of Oakhaven for him and his brother.
- **Need:** To realize that a life without memories is not a life at all, and that his brother needs him whole, not hollowed.
- **Sliders:** Proactivity (7/10), Likability (6/10), Competence (8/10). He is highly skilled at extracting the maximum magical output from the smallest, most mundane memories.
- **Arc:** Positive. He starts out transactional and cold, willing to burn anything. He ends up refusing to burn a crucial memory to save himself, choosing to keep his humanity instead.
- **Speech pattern:** Terse, transactional, avoids adjectives. Short sentences.
  - *Example:* "I need a fire. I'll trade you the taste of that apple from yesterday. Just light it."
- **Secrets:** He doesn't just burn his own memories; he has found a way to "skim" the emotional residue off others' spells, stealing their emotional resonance without burning the memory itself. This is highly illegal.
- **Relationships:**
  - **Taran (brother):** Deeply protective, but emotionally distant because he has burned so many childhood memories of them playing together.
  - **Silas (mentor):** Relies on him for work, but distrusts his connections to the Cinder Syndicate.

## Taran Vance (Non-POV)
- **Role:** Elian's younger brother, a Maker in training.
- **Age:** 14
- **Ghost:** Watching Elian slowly turn cold and distant over the years.
- **Wound:** He feels responsible for Elian's hollowing, knowing his brother burned memories to keep him alive.
- **Lie:** "If I can create enough beautiful, intense experiences for Elian, he won't have to burn any more of his past."
- **Want:** To become a Master Maker and build a sensory palace for Elian.
- **Need:** To understand that he cannot replace the specific love Elian burned away; he can only offer new love.
- **Sliders:** Proactivity (4/10), Likability (9/10), Competence (3/10). He is naive and struggles with the physical toll of Maker training.
- **Arc:** Flat. He represents the emotional anchor Elian needs to hold onto.
- **Speech pattern:** Hopeful, descriptive, uses many sensory words. Tends to ask questions.
  - *Example:* "Did you see the color of the sky today? It looked like bruised iron. Almost purple. You could use that, couldn't you?"
- **Secrets:** He has been secretly selling his own minor memories (like the taste of honey, the feeling of rain) to buy food for them, hiding the gaps in his memory from Elian.
- **Relationships:**
  - **Elian:** Loves him desperately, but is terrified of him.

## Silas Thorne (Non-POV)
- **Role:** Fence for the Cinder Syndicate, Elian's employer.
- **Age:** 55
- **Ghost:** He burned the memory of his entire marriage to fund his rise in the Syndicate.
- **Wound:** He has a house full of a woman's belongings and no idea who she was or why he kept them.
- **Lie:** "Power is the only thing that can't be burned away."
- **Want:** To overthrow the Keepers and legalize the trade of stolen memories.
- **Need:** To accept the grief of what he has lost, rather than trying to fill the void with control over others.
- **Sliders:** Proactivity (9/10), Likability (2/10), Competence (9/10). A ruthless, highly effective operator.
- **Arc:** Negative. He doubles down on his lie, eventually burning his own empathy to achieve a political goal.
- **Speech pattern:** Manipulative, uses leading questions, rarely raises his voice. Uses metaphors related to commerce and banking.
  - *Example:* "You're spending too much capital on sentiment, Elian. The market demands a steeper price. What else can you afford to lose?"
- **Secrets:** He knows how to force a memory burn from another person, the ultimate taboo.
- **Relationships:**
  - **Elian:** Sees him as a useful tool, but expendable.
  - **The Keepers:** Hates them for their hypocrisy.

## Magistrate Vane (Non-POV)
- **Role:** Antagonist, a high-ranking Keeper in Oakhaven.
- **Age:** 40
- **Ghost:** She witnessed the aftermath of a Hollowed riot when she was a child.
- **Wound:** A deep-seated terror of chaos and the loss of self-control.
- **Lie:** "Strict regulation of memory is the only thing keeping the city from tearing itself apart."
- **Want:** To root out the Cinder Syndicate and implement a mandatory "Memory Tithe" for all citizens.
- **Need:** To realize that her rigid control is creating the very Hollowed she fears.
- **Sliders:** Proactivity (8/10), Likability (3/10), Competence (7/10). Highly capable bureaucrat, lacking empathy.
- **Arc:** Flat. She remains rigid in her beliefs, serving as the immovable object against Elian's desperately fluid morality.
- **Speech pattern:** Precise, formal, uses complex vocabulary. Never uses contractions.
  - *Example:* "The law is not a suggestion, Elian. It is the structure upon which survival is built. You have compromised that structure."
- **Secrets:** She has a private Maker who designs extremely painful experiences for her, which she then burns to fuel her immense magical wards around the city. She is essentially torturing herself for the state.
- **Relationships:**
  - **Elian:** Sees him as a symptom of a diseased society.
  - **Silas:** Her primary target and philosophical opposite.
