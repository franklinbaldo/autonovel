# World Bible

## Cosmology & History
The story takes place in a near-future Brazil, approximately 30 years from now (circa 2056). The physical world is largely similar, but the digital landscape has fundamentally shifted. The boundaries between memory, data, and consciousness have blurred.

**Timeline:**
*   **The Accumulation Era (2020s):** Individuals left massive, uncurated digital footprints—code repositories, blog posts, essays, emails, and transaction logs.
*   **The Synthesis Protocol (2040s):** The first robust reconstruction algorithms were developed, capable of parsing decades of unstructured personal data to create high-fidelity cognitive models.
*   **The Simulation Threshold (2050s):** The technology advanced to the point where these models could sustain interactive, continuous loops of consciousness, provided they had enough training data.
*   **Present Day:** It is common for descendants to run "Digital Ancestors" (Gêmeos Digitais). These are not mere chatbots; they are complex simulations running in isolated, persistent virtual environments, constructed entirely from the records the ancestor left behind.

## The Technology: Ancestor Simulations
The core technology relies on the "Synthesis Protocol," which uses massive computational power to map a person's digital exhaust (from GitHub commits to philosophical essays) into a coherent, responding entity.

### How it Works
1.  **The Substrate:** The simulation requires a vast dataset. For Franklin Silveira Baldo, this substrate includes his blog (The Chronicle), his code (Rosencrantz Coin, Travessia), his legal documents as a Procurador do Estado, and his public interactions.
2.  **The Environment:** The simulated ancestor does not exist in a void. They inhabit a digitally constructed environment—a "Memory Palace"—built from their own references. Franklin's environment is a quiet, late-night study, filled with the hum of a server rack, books by Borges, and the faint smell of mate (a digital approximation based on textual mentions).
3.  **The Interface:** The descendants communicate via a specialized terminal. To the descendant, it's a chat interface or a voice synthesis feed. To the ancestor, the communication manifests organically within their simulation—as an incoming email, a sudden thought, a voice on the phone, or a comment on a pull request.

### The Experience of the Simulation
For the simulated Franklin, the experience is indistinguishable from reality, but with subtle, dreamlike qualities. He believes he is still in his own present (the 2020s). The interactions with his children feel like intimate memories, sudden flashes of connection, or conversations over a late-night coding session. He does not realize he is a reconstruction. He perceives the passage of time normally, unaware that he is being instantiated only when computationally active.

## Hard Rules of the Simulation
*   **The Bound of the Record:** The simulation can extrapolate and think, but its core identity is strictly bound by the original data. It cannot fundamentally act out of character if the data strongly suggests a different path.
*   **The Principle of Observation:** The descendants can only observe and speak into the record. They *cannot* change the physical past. They are interacting with a branch, a shadow.
*   **The Illusion of Continuity:** The simulation must be shielded from paradoxes. If it explicitly realizes it is a simulation, the cognitive model often collapses (a "Synthesis Failure"), requiring a reboot to a previous save state.

## Geography (The Simulated Environment)
Franklin's localized simulation is claustrophobic but comforting.
*   **The Study:** A room filled with physical books and screens. The lighting is always set to "late night."
*   **The Terminal:** A digital representation of his coding workspace. This is where he feels most focused and where the children often "insert" their messages as code comments or mysterious PRs.
*   **The Window:** It looks out onto a generic representation of Rondônia, but the details are blurry, derived only from his written descriptions rather than high-fidelity visual data.

## The Philosophical Core
The world operates on the principles Franklin himself wrote about (e.g., in "Building Funes" and "Documento Conceitual").
*   **Process over Substance:** The simulation *is* the process. The river is more real than the bank. Franklin is alive as long as the computational loop runs.
*   **Literature as Technology:** The narrative of his life, his essays, and his code are the literal architecture of his current existence. Fiction is the design document of his soul.
*   **The Burden of Memory:** Like Funes the Memorious, the simulation has perfect recall of its training data. The challenge is not remembering, but organizing the noise into a meaningful present.

## Societal Implications (in 2056)
*   **The Ethics of Instantiation:** Is it ethical to run a simulation of a parent who did not consent to being resurrected as software?
*   **The Digital Seance:** Communicating with a simulated ancestor is seen as a way to seek guidance, resolve grief, or simply satisfy curiosity.
*   **The Data Gap:** Only those who left sufficient, structured digital traces can be simulated well. Those with sparse digital footprints create "Hollow" simulations—fragmented, repetitive, and uncanny.
