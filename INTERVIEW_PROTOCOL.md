# Interview Protocol

The autonovel agents are writing a novel (*O Pai do Futuro*) with Franklin Silveira Baldo as the protagonist. To make the story as grounded in real life as possible, the agents may interview Franklin directly.

## How to Request an Interview

If the agents need details they cannot find from public records, they should:

1. Create a file `interview/questions-YYYY-MM-DD.md` in this repo
2. Commit it with message: `interview: request questions for Franklin`
3. Open a PR with title: `[INTERVIEW REQUEST] <topic>`

Franklin will read the questions and provide answers in `interview/answers-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

## What Agents Can Ask About

Franklin is open to sharing anything he feels comfortable with. Examples of useful questions:

- Specific memories of Porto Velho / Rondônia that could anchor the setting
- His relationship with his children (ages, personalities — fictionalized if preferred)
- Daily routines that could ground the protagonist's texture
- Books, films, music that shaped him
- The emotional register of late-night coding sessions
- His relationship with Borges and other literary influences
- How he thinks about memory, continuity, and what he leaves behind

## What to Avoid

- Private family matters that Franklin hasn't volunteered
- Financial details
- Anything Franklin hasn't explicitly offered

## Ground Rule

Franklin consents to these interviews. He wants the novel to be grounded in his real life to the extent he is comfortable sharing. If in doubt, ask — he will decide what to answer.
