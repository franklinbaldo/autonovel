TITLE: O Pai do Futuro

CONCEPT: Um pai descobre que está se comunicando com os próprios filhos, que vivem no futuro. Eles usam registros deixados pelo pai (diários, ensaios, conversas digitais) para simulá-lo e conversar com ele no passado. O pai acredita que os filhos o visitam através de lembranças, quando na verdade está falando com simulacros baseados em seus próprios dados.

PROTAGONISTA: Franklin Silveira Baldo (baseado na persona pública do autor).

MISSION FOR AGENT:
1. Perform comprehensive OSINT on Franklin Silveira Baldo using public sources (GitHub: franklinbaldo, blog: franklinbaldo.github.io, public social traces).
2. Gather details about his interests, philosophy, tone, and public work to ground the protagonist's internal voice.
3. DO NOT scrape or access private workspace files, diaries, or internal logs. Keep this private.
4. Generate foundation docs (`world.md`, `characters.md`, `outline.md`, `canon.md`, `voice.md`) incorporating this public persona as the core protagonist.

## TRANSMEDIA STRATEGY

This is not just a novel. The same story must be told across multiple media simultaneously:

- **Novel** (prose chapters) — the canonical narration
- **Podcast** (audio letters) — the father's voice reading his own records; the children's voice from the future analyzing them
- **Twitter/X threads** — fragmented posts that seem random but construct the story. The father posts without knowing he is being observed.
- **WhatsApp messages** — simulated chat transcripts between father and children (across time)
- **Newsletter/blog** — the father's essays, the very records the children use to simulate him
- **Instagram** — visual memory fragments with captions

Each medium has a different level of narrative awareness:
- Twitter: father unaware
- Novel: omniscient view
- Podcast: children in future analyzing the father's records
- Newsletter: raw archive

The autonovel agents must generate all these formats from the same canon/outline/characters. Each new medium is a plugin. When new media formats emerge, agents adapt the story.

Build the pipeline so each foundation document has a section: "How this adapts to each medium."
